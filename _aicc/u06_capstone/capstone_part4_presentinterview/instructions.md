The Interview

The interview is a 5-10 minute session where you presents your work and answer questions from the instructor. If you worked with a partner, each partner will be interviewed SEPARATELY. The goal is to ensure a deep understanding of the technical, ethical, and practical aspects of their models.

Let Mr. Riley know when you are ready to do "The Interview."
